◎ 上声 shǎngshēng

[one of the four tones in Chinese pronunciation] 四声之一,上声( shàngshēng)的又音

上声高呼猛烈强。——真空《玉钥匙歌诀》

◎ 上声 shàngshēng

(1) [the second of the four tones in classical Chinese and the third tone in modern standard Chinese pronunciation]

(2) 古汉语四声的第二声

(3) 普通话字调的第三声
